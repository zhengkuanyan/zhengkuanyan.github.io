ChineseMe

Chinese Language Online Learning Website

ChineseMe is an online learning website designed for beginners and committed learners to learn authentic Chinese language. Their courses are episode-based. I worked for them as a UX designers to design an effective learning experience for each episode. I was responsible for interaction design and interface design and conducting user testing using prototype. This website is under development now and will soon be launched.

THE BACKGROUND

Learning Chinese is Becoming Trendy

As China's economy is growing faster than ever, learning Chinese has never been so trendy. ChineseMe is one of the players in the online Chinese learning market that is devoted to bringing authentic value to people learning Chinese. Since June 2017, ChineseMe started to create its online Chinese learning product and I collaborated with them as a product designer to launch the first version of the website.

THE PRODUCT

Introducing ChineseMe

ChineseMe is an online learning course website for beginners to learn Chinese. Users can go through well-designed course episodes and learn Chinese step by step from characters, words, grammar, dialogs, to exercise.

Develop your learning pathway through well-designed episodes

Users can develop systematic language skills as they study each episode that features certain topic.


Play a role in dialogs study

Users can explore each sentence of dialogs presented by various personalities, and play one of the roles to strengthen their speaking skills.

Look up new words anytime

Users can look up new words in the words library anytime they don’t understand a sentence.

Test your understanding

Users can test their understanding of knowledge in each episode through exercise, and get instant feedback on their answers.

Track your learning progress

Users can track their learning progress in details in our “map”. They can also go to any element in an episode if they want to review specific language point.

THE VISUAL LANGUAGE

Make Design Consistent and Pleasing

UI kits to increase consistency

I developed a set of UI elements to make the design of the whole website more consistent. I tried to strike a balance between a feeling of "professional" and "delightful". So as for the choice of style, I avoided too childish and gamified design, but at the same time not too rigid and boring. And these UI elements served as a good tool to communicate with the developer.

Eposode illustrations incease pleasure

Also, I created illustrations for each episode according to the theme of that episode in order to better present the basic idea of the content. Users also found that this design gave them a bit more incentives to unlock the new episodes.

THE OUTCOME

Getting Ready for Launch

After I finished final interface design, this project went to development. The product is now in beta. They invited more users to the testing site and receiving feedback on improvement.
